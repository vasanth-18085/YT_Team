# TASK 14 — About Section

## Channel Description (2,500 characters max — YouTube About section)

```
Vyasa's Witness

The Mahabharata you know was shaped by the Star Plus TV show, popular retellings, and secondhand mythology. 

This channel reads the source.

Everything here is from the BORI Critical Edition — the Bhandarkar Oriental Research Institute's scholarly text, compiled from the oldest surviving manuscripts, translated into English by Bibek Debroy. When we say "the Mahabharata says," we mean a specific parva, a specific section, a specific verse.

What this channel covers:
• BORI Corrections — what the text actually records vs. what popular culture says
• Character Studies — Karna, Bhishma, Draupadi, Yudhishthira, and others, read from the source
• Mysteries & Historical Evidence — Dwarka, Kurukshetra astronomy, the text's geography
• Philosophy Deep Dives — dharma, karma, free will, the Gita in context
• Power Scaling & VS — warrior rankings as Bhishma himself stated them in the Udyoga Parva
• What-If Simulations — counterfactual readings grounded in the text's own logic
• The Complete Mahabharata — all 18 parvas narrated in order, from the BORI source

Who this is for:
If you grew up with the Mahabharata but want to know what it actually says, you're in the right place.
If you've read the Gita and want to understand the full text it comes from, you're in the right place.
If you're frustrated by mythology content that never cites its sources, you're in the right place.

Upload schedule: Every Saturday, 8:00 AM IST

This channel is independent. No sponsors yet. Everything is built from the text.
```

**Word count:** ~235 words (well under the 2,500 character limit) ✅
**Note:** Keep it factual, mission-focused, and audience-clear. No hype.

**Typography guidance:**
- Channel name: Large, commanding, serif — think EB Garamond, Cormorant Garamond, or Cinzel
