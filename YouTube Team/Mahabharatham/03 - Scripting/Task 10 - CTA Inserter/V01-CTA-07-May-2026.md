## CTA Strategy for Vyasa's Witness

This is a new channel. CTAs should be:
- **Low-friction** — subscribe asks should feel natural, not desperate
- **Mission-aligned** — "Subscribe because the next video corrects another misconception" > "Subscribe if you liked this"
- **Community-building** — comments should be invited for genuine debate, not just engagement farming
- **Patreon-ready** — set up the concept early, launch the link at 1K subs
