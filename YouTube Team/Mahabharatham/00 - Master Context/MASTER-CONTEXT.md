## 7. Myths & Bad Common Advice

1. **"The Mahabharata is mainly the Pandava-Kaurava war story."** Wrong. The war is ~5 of 18 parvas. The Shantiparva alone is 3,000+ shlokas on governance. The text is vastly more than its climax.
2. **"Karna was the greatest warrior in the Mahabharata."** The BORI text (Udyoga Parva) has Bhishma explicitly rate Karna as an Ardha-Rathi — half a chariot fighter — below full Maharathi status. The text itself defines the hierarchy.
3. **"Barbarika / Khatushyamji is in the Mahabharata."** Absent from all 18 parvas of the BORI Critical Edition. This is a regional folk tradition — not the text.
4. **"Just watch the Star Plus show — it covers everything."** The show invents characters, compresses events, alters motivations, and omits entire parvas. It is not the text.
5. **"Krishna was openly a god throughout the war."** The BORI text is internally inconsistent on this. Krishna functions as a strategist for most of the war. His divine nature is revealed selectively, and differently in different sections.
6. **"Draupadi was humiliated because she laughed at Duryodhana in Maya's palace."** The Sabha Parva does not establish this causal chain. The text records the laughter but does not state it caused the dice game. This is popular interpretation, not text.
