## Phase 3 (10K–25K subs): Inbound Begins

By 10K subs with a diaspora-heavy audience, sponsors will start to approach you. At this point:
1. Don't take the first offer — compare against rate card
2. Only work with brands that fit naturally (you would genuinely recommend this)
3. NEVER run a sponsor that contradicts the channel's scholarship standard (no "astrology apps" etc.)
4. Maximum 1 sponsored video per 4 uploads — maintain the channel's integrity ratio
