## FIIRE Rationale for This Channel

**Tinder** → BORI Corrections (shocking facts about beloved characters) + Power Scaling (Karna debates, warrior rankings) — these are algorithmically hot RIGHT NOW in mythology/history content.

**Kindling** → Character Studies (consistent character searches, monthly demand) + Mystery/Historical (archaeology, Dwarka — perennial interest).

**Logs** → Parva Narration series (evergreen backbone — "Mahabharata Adi Parva" gets searched every week forever) + Philosophy Deep Dives (niche but permanent demand from Gita readers).

**Flint** → What-If Simulations (no proven search demand, but high shareability and comment engagement potential — experimental).

## Key FIIRE Insights for Vyasa's Witness

1. **Tinder content = BORI Corrections + Power Scaling** — the Karna ranking and Eklavya death videos will be the discovery engine for this channel. These need to stay in heavy rotation in Year 1 (every 4 weeks).

2. **Logs backbone = Parva Narration** — this is the reason people SUBSCRIBE and STAY. Each new parva episode adds a permanent evergreen asset. OneTimelessWitness proves 10 deep videos can drive 332K views/month — the Parva series is your permanent engine.

3. **Flint calibration** — What-If videos are fun but have no proven search demand. Keep them as the 1-in-4 rotation, not back-to-back. If a What-If video underperforms, replace it with a Kindling Character Study in that slot.

4. **Timing matters** — per SeeUinHistory and ReligionCamp data, Saturday uploads at 8 AM IST (Friday 9:30 PM EST) maximize CTR for diaspora audiences. Stick to this.
