## SEAS Framework (Quick Reference)

| Dimension | What It Measures | Score Range |
|-----------|-----------------|------------|
| **S — Spread of Users** | How many people search for / would discover this topic? | 1–10 |
| **E — Excitement** | Does the title and concept create emotional pull to click? | 1–10 |
| **A — Audience Match** | Does this video serve our ICP (Indian diaspora, philosophy readers, BORI seekers)? | 1–10 |
| **S — Simplicity** | Is the core value proposition immediately clear in the title? | 1–10 |

**Threshold:** Total ≥ 30/40 = Approved. 35–40 = Priority launch. Below 30 = Reconsider or reframe.

---

## CCN Fit (Core / Casual / New) — Validation Layer

| Viewer Type | Description | What They Need |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Core** | Already follows Mahabharata content deeply. Wants BORI accuracy. | Corrections, citations, complexity |
| **Casual** | Knows the characters vaguely. Watches mythology occasionally. | Character access, familiar names, storytelling |
| **New** | No Mahabharata background. Arrived via philosophy/history/mystery. | Clear context, universal themes, no assumed knowledge |

Each video should serve at least 2 of 3 viewer types.
