## Part 4 — What They ALL Get Wrong (The Universal Gap)

### 1. Nobody validates properly
Every single competitor (except QuantPy on theoretical level) either doesn't discuss validation or uses simple train/test splits that leak future information. Walk-forward validation with purge + embargo is STANDARD in production quant but **zero channels teach it**. This is your Video 2 — the myth-buster that establishes credibility.

### 2. Nobody shows the full pipeline
Every competitor shows fragments:
- Part Time Larry → API connections
- Algovibes → individual strategy backtests
- Nicholas Renotte → model training
- Ryan O'Connell → broker integration

Nobody shows: data → features → model → signals → sentiment → fusion → risk → position sizing → backtest → production hardening → results. Your system does, in 10 phases.

### 3. Nobody is honest about results
"I made $X with my trading bot" is the dominant narrative. Nobody shows: "Here's where my model failed, here's the realistic Sharpe after costs, here's the drawdown." Your Videos 21-22 (market impact + realistic backtesting) directly attack this blind spot.

### 4. Nobody teaches modern ML for finance
LSTM stock prediction is 2018-era content. Nobody covers: PatchTST, TFT, iTransformer, Chronos, DLinear, N-BEATS, N-HiTS, TiDE. Your Videos 7-9 bring 2024-2025 ML to quant finance.

### 5. Nobody does NLP for trading signals properly
Sentdex touched NLP basics years ago. Nobody has: multiple fine-tuned transformer models, LoRA, honest sentiment impact measurement, daily aggregation pipeline. Your Videos 4, 11-12 own this space.
