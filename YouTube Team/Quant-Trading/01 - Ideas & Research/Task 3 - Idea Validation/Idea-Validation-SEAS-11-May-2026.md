## SEAS Dimension Key

| Dimension | What It Measures |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **S — Spread of Users** | How many *types* of people would click — ML engineers, SWEs, quant students, traders |
| **E — Excitement** | Emotional pull, thumbnail potential, "I have to watch this" factor |
| **A — Audience Match** | Directly relevant to the MLQuant core audience (ML/SWE entering quant) |
| **S2 — Simplicity** | How easy to package as a coherent title/hook without losing meaning |
