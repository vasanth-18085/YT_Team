## PART 2 — ON-CAMERA PREP PLAN (TEACH Framework)

### MLQuant Camera Style
- **Positioning:** Teacher (documented system builder)
- **Energy:** Calm-confident (5-6/10) — fits deep technical explainer niche
- **Format:** Faceless channel with voiceover + screen recording + diagrams
- **Note:** Even faceless channels benefit from camera drills for voice quality

### TEACH Self-Review Criteria

| Letter | Criterion | MLQuant Application |
|--------|-----------|---------------------|
| **T** — Tone | Confident, fitting to topic? | Authoritative but not arrogant. Match script's "honest engineer" voice. |
| **E** — Eye Contact | N/A (faceless) | Substitute: **Voice engagement** — are you talking TO someone or reading AT them? |
| **A** — Animation | Hand gestures natural? | Substitute: **Vocal variety** — pitch changes, emphasis on key terms, natural pauses |
| **C** — Coherence | Clear structure, good examples? | Does the explanation build logically? Could a first-time viewer follow? |
| **H** — Haste | Speed right, pauses used? | Technical content needs slower pace. Pause after key definitions. |

### Voice Recording Prep (Per Session)

**Warm-Up (5 min before recording):**
1. Read a section of the script out loud at 7/10 energy (2 min)
2. Record yourself explaining one concept without the script (2 min)
3. Listen back — check for mumbling, rushing, monotone (1 min)

**Delivery Rules:**
- Bullets, not scripts — use the script as a guide, speak naturally
- Clap or snap before re-starting a sentence (editing marker)
- "Think aloud" on tough concepts rather than reading stiffly
- Pause 1 second after every defined jargon term
- Vary sentence length: short punchy statement, then explanation

**Post-Recording TEACH Review:**
- Listen to every recording twice
- Rate each TEACH criterion 1-5
- Note one fix to apply next session
- Track improvement across sessions

### Weekly Practice Schedule (Pre-Launch)

| Day | Activity | Duration |
|-----|----------|----------|
| Mon | Everyday Drill: narrate today's work on the system | 10 min |
| Tue | Practice record one script section (discard output) | 20 min |
| Wed | Rapid Fire: explain random concepts from the 26 videos | 10 min |
| Thu | Record real content + TEACH review | 60 min |
| Fri | Listen to Thursday's recording, note fixes | 15 min |

### Milestones

| Phase | When | Target |
|-------|------|--------|
| Baseline | Week 1 | Record V0 trailer draft. Review with TEACH. Identify top weakness. |
| Comfort | Week 3 | Can record 10-min sections without stopping. Voice sounds natural. |
| Consistency | Week 5 | All recordings rate 3+ on all TEACH criteria. Delivery speed stable. |
| Launch | Week 6 | V0 and V1 fully recorded, edited, and ready to publish. |
