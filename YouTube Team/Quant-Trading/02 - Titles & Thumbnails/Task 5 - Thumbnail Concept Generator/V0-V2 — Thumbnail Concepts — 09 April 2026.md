### Design Notes for All Thumbnails
- **Color palette:** Dark backgrounds (navy/charcoal), accent glows (green for positive, red for fear, gold for premium)
- **Typography:** Bold, sans-serif, all-caps. White or bright accent color. Left-aligned.
- **Layout:** Text left (30-40% width), visual right (60-70% width)
- **Faceless channel rule:** No real faces. Use silhouettes, objects, diagrams, abstract visuals.
- **Readability test:** Must be legible at mobile phone size (120×67px equivalent)
