## Visual Continuity Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Background** | Always #0d1117 or #1a1a2e dark. Never white or grey backgrounds. |
| **Accent** | Gold #FFD700 for key text, arrows, highlights, boxes. Consistent throughout S1. |
| **Face expression** | Match to content: curious for theories, focused for builds, honest for results. Not hype-face. |
| **Text max** | 5 words in largest text size. Secondary text max 8 words. Never 3 lines. |
| **Project arc** | P1 thumbnails slightly simpler → P7 thumbnails show complexity growth (visual series narrative) |
