## Design Notes for Thumbnail Production

### Consistent Elements Across All 26 Thumbnails
- **Background:** Dark (near-black or dark navy/charcoal). Never white or bright.
- **Text placement:** Left side, large, bold sans-serif font (Impact, Bebas Neue, or Montserrat Black).
- **Text color:** White or bright yellow/gold. Occasional red for warning/danger thumbnails.
- **Visual placement:** Right side or center-right. One focal point max.
- **Color accents:** Teal/cyan for tech, green for positive/success, red for danger/failure, gold for premium/winner.
- **Brand accent colour: GOLD (#FFD700 / warm gold).** This is the MLQuant signature — used on hero/winner thumbnails (V7, V11, V14, V25), the cracking padlock (V0), and wherever the 44-model system is the protagonist. Every thumbnail should include at least a subtle gold element (text glow, accent line, icon highlight) for brand consistency across the 26-video series.
- **No human faces.** Use objects, diagrams, charts, icons, metaphorical visuals.
- **No clutter.** If you can't understand it in 1 second at phone size, simplify.

### Visual Motifs That Repeat (Intentionally)
- **Before/After splits** — V2, V5, V12, V14, V20, V22 (transformation is the series promise)
- **Shattered/broken things** — V2, V8, V22, V24 (myth-busting energy)
- **Tournament/competition** — V7, V11, V16, V25 (head-to-head comparisons)
- **Funnel/pipeline diagrams** — V5, V10, V17, V23 (system-building videos)

### Thumbnail-Title Pair Verification
Every pair should pass this test:
1. ✅ Does the thumbnail add NEW information the title doesn't have?
2. ✅ Does the title + thumbnail together create a knowledge gap only the video fills?
3. ✅ Would the ideal viewer (ML-aware, zero QF, mid-20s) stop scrolling?
4. ✅ Is it readable at phone size in under 1 second?
