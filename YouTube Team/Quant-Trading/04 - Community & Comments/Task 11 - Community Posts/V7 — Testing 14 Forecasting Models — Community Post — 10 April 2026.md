**Date:** 10 April 2026

I benchmarked 14 forecasting models under identical conditions.

ARIMA. Prophet. LSTM. GRU. TCN. DLinear. N-BEATS. TiDE. PatchTST. TFT. iTransformer. Chronos.

Same walk forward folds. Same embargo. Same metrics.

The results surprised me. Prophet lost to ARIMA. DLinear achieved 86% of TFT's Sharpe at 3% of the compute cost. And a pre trained foundation model with zero training beat every recurrent architecture.
