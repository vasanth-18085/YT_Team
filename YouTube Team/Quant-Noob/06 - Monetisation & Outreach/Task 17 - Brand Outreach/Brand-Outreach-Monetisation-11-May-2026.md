## Revenue Philosophy

The Quant Noob earns revenue the same way MLQuant does — as augmentation to the Dharma plan, not the plan itself. Zoho salary funds the transition. YouTube income builds on top. The "free always" rule is the differentiator, not a sacrifice.

**Revenue reality for this channel:**
- This is a learning-journey channel, not a project-tutorial channel.
- CPM in this niche is good ($4–12 for English quant/finance content) but views will be lower than MLQuant in Year 1 because the content is more personal.
- Honest expectation: ₹0 in Year 1. ₹2–5K/month in Year 2. The revenue upside of this channel is community-driven (memberships, affiliates) not view-driven.

**Target by scenario (from Dharma plan, combined Quant Noob + MLQuant estimate):**
- Conservative: ₹2K/month by 2028 from Quant Noob alone
- Realistic: ₹6K/month by 2028 (affiliates + membership + AdSense)
- Optimistic: ₹12K/month by 2029 if audience hits 5K+ engaged subscribers

## Revenue Stream 3 — AdSense (Post 1,000 Subs + 4,000 Watch Hours)

English-language quant/finance learning content in this niche has strong CPMs from US/UK/Canada traffic.

| Metric | Estimate |
|--------|---------|
| CPM (India traffic) | $0.46 (~₹38) per 1,000 views |
| CPM (US/UK traffic) | $4–12 (~₹330–990) per 1,000 views |
| Likely audience mix | 40% India, 35% US/UK/CA, 25% other |
| Blended RPM | ~$1.5–3 (~₹125–250) per 1,000 views |
| At 10K views/month | ₹1,250–2,500/month |
| At 30K views/month | ₹3,750–7,500/month |

**Honest verdict:** AdSense is not the engine here. It's the baseline reward for having done the work. The affiliate and membership streams will outperform AdSense until the channel is at 20K+ views/month.

**Monetisation eligibility target:** May 2027 (12 months from launch). Need 1,000 subs + 4,000 watch hours. Realistic if V01–V10 are published and each gets 300–500 views.

## Revenue Stream 4 — YouTube Membership (Post 2,000 Subs)

3 tiers. All tiers are community-based — no member-exclusive technical content. Technical content stays free forever.

| Tier | Price | Perks |
|------|-------|-------|
| **Tier 1 — The Learner** | ₹99/month | Badge, access to monthly "what I'm studying this month" member post, name in video credits |
| **Tier 2 — The Builder** | ₹299/month | All above + monthly Q&A comment thread (Vasanth answers in thread) |
| **Tier 3 — The Quant Noob** | ₹499/month | All above + priority comment reply, listed in "Quant Noob Community" pinned post |

**Why ₹499 for Tier 3 (not ₹999 like MLQuant):** This channel's audience skews toward active learners who may be students or early-career — price sensitivity is higher. ₹499/month is meaningful but not prohibitive.

**Honest projection:** At 2,000 subs, 2% conversion = 40 members. Mix: 25×₹99 + 12×₹299 + 3×₹499 = ₹2,475 + ₹3,588 + ₹1,497 = ~₹7,500/month. Real. Not life-changing. But it compounds.

**When to launch:** After 2,000 subscribers. Don't rush it — the membership needs social proof to convert.

## Sponsorship Strategy

**Timeline:** No sponsorships until Year 2 (May 2027 onward) and only after V10+ are published. Before that, the channel doesn't have enough proof of audience to approach sponsors with confidence.

**Sponsorship philosophy:** Maximum 1 sponsor per video. 60–90 second read max. Fully disclosed. Only brands relevant to the learning journey.

### Tier 1 — Learning Tools (First Sponsorships — Year 2)
| Company | Fit | Approach Window |
|---------|-----|-----------------|
| **QuantInsti / EPAT** | Already affiliated — can convert to full sponsor at scale | Post 2K subs, post V05 published |
| **Zerodha Kite Connect** | NSE trading API — Indian audience, directly relevant | Post V19 (market data engine) |
| **Alpaca Markets** | Paper trading API — free tier works, great for beginners | Post V20 (backtesting video) |
| **GitHub (Student/Pro)** | Developer-first audience — GitHub copilot pitch | Year 2 |

### Tier 2 — Learning Platforms (Later)
| Company | Fit | When |
|---------|-----|------|
| Brilliant.org | Math + CS learning — fits the quant study angle | Year 2+ |
| Coursera | Only if they're hosting a quant-adjacent credential | Year 2+ |
| Notion | Study system video (V10) — note-taking tool | Year 2 with second edition |

**Rate guideline:**
- Under 5K subs: no sponsorship rates yet. Focus on affiliates.
- 5K subs: ₹15,000–25,000 per integration
- 10K subs: ₹30,000–50,000 per integration
