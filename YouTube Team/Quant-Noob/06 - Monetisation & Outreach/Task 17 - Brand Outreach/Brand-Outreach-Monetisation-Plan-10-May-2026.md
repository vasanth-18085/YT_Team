**Strategy to reach 4,000 watch hours:**
- V01 (channel trailer) + V10 (study system) are highest-retention candidates — promote these first

## DISCLOSURE LANGUAGE (Standard Blocks)

**Video description — affiliate books:**
> 📚 Books I mention (Amazon affiliate links — I earn a small commission if you buy, at no extra cost to you):

**Video description — course affiliate:**
> 🎓 If you're interested in [CPF/EPAT] and want to sign up, I have a referral link: [link]. I earn a commission if you use it — full disclosure.

**Sponsored segment script line:**
> "This video is [partly / sponsored by] [Company]. [One honest sentence about what they do and why I think they're relevant to you]. Check the link in the description if you want to try it."

**Key:** Never claim to have personally tested something you haven't. Never endorse a product you wouldn't use yourself.
