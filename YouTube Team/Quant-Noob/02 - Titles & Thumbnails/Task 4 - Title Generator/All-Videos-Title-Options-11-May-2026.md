## Title Framework Notes

**What works for this channel:**
- First-person voice ("I", "My") — channels the learning-in-public identity
- Honesty signals ("Honest", "Real", "What I actually learned") — differentiates from guru channels
- Contrast and failure arcs ("Perfect then didn't", "What they don't tell you") — high E
- Beginner anchoring ("No PhD Required", "For Beginners") on Logs videos — captures the long-tail search

**What to avoid:**
- Hype ("AMAZING", "LIFE-CHANGING", "MASTER") — breaks the honest brand
- Oversimplification ("Easy guide", "In 5 minutes") — the content is not that
- Generic ("Python tutorial") — too broad, no differentiation
