---

## 5C FRAMEWORK (Applied Per Video)

| C | Check | Pass Criteria |
|---|-------|--------------|
| **Composition** | Framing and visual hierarchy | Face clearly visible, text not fighting with face, clear focal point |
| **Context** | 18% glance test — understood at a glance | A person scrolling at normal speed can understand the subject in <2 seconds |
| **Clean** | ≤3 elements total | Face + text + 1 supporting element max. No clutter. |
| **Curiosity** | Knowledge gap exists | Viewer must want to know something they don't already know |
| **Color** | Brand accent used | #58a6ff (electric blue) or #ffffff dominant, #0d1117 background |

---

## THUMBNAIL EMOTION GUIDE

| Video type | Expression | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| Personal milestone (passed exam, year recap) | Genuine satisfaction / quiet pride | Not over-the-top. Real. |
| Failure / honest post-mortem | Slight wince or rueful smile | Authentic. Not dramatized crying face. |
| Concept explainer | "I get it now" look — calm clarity | Not lecture face. Discovery energy. |
| Build-in-public | Focused / flow state | Engaged with screen, not at camera |
| Career / advice | Direct eye contact — confident | Speaking directly to the viewer |

## THUMBNAIL DO'S AND DON'TS

| ✅ DO | ❌ DON'T |
|------|---------|
| Use genuine facial expressions | Pose with fake smile or exaggerated shock |
| Keep background dark and consistent | Use random or inconsistent backgrounds |
| Max 3 elements per thumbnail | Add logos, icons, watermarks, extra text |
| Use #58a6ff for electric blue accents | Use yellow/orange (too clickbait for this channel) |
| Allow background texture (charts, code) to be faint | Put sharp, readable elements in background |
| Match expression to the video's emotional truth | Use the same expression for every thumbnail |
