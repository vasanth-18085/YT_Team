## FIIRE DEFINITIONS (for this channel)

| Category | Definition | Examples |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **Tinder** | Timely, event-driven, viral potential — personal milestones, hot topics in AI/quant | "I passed my EPAT", "I failed a quant interview", "AI in finance 2026" |
| **Kindling** | Core educational + narrative-building — drives the series arc, brings back subscribers | "CPF explained", "How I study after work", "Linear algebra for quant" |
| **Logs** | Evergreen — discovered 2 years later and still relevant | "What is quant finance?", "Sharpe ratio explained", "What does a quant dev do?" |
| **Flint** | Experimental — livestreams, build sessions, format tests, might flop but worth testing | "Study-with-me live", "I built X in 3 hours", "Day in my life" |
