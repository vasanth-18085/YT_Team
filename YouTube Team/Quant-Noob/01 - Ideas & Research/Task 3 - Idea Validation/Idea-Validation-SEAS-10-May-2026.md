## SEAS SCORING GUIDE (This Channel)

| Dimension | What it means for The Quant Noob | Score 1–10 |
|-----------|----------------------------------|------------|
| **S — Spread of Users** | How wide is the potential audience? Does this reach beyond just hardcore quant learners? | Broad appeal = 8–10, Niche = 4–6 |
| **E — Excitement** | Does the topic create emotional resonance? Is there a curiosity gap, a relatable pain, a surprising angle? | High = 8–10, Low = 4–6 |
| **A — Audience Match** | Does this serve our specific ICP (ML/SWE engineers on the quant path)? | Perfect fit = 9–10, Adjacent = 5–7 |
| **S — Simplicity** | Can it be executed well in 20–35 min without being oversimplified? | Clean scope = 8–10, Scope creep risk = 4–7 |

**CCN Fit:** Every video should be mappable to at least one of:
- **Core viewer:** Already on the quant learning path, watches every video
- **Casual viewer:** Quant-curious, might subscribe after one video
- **New viewer:** First-time discovery via search, potential to become core
