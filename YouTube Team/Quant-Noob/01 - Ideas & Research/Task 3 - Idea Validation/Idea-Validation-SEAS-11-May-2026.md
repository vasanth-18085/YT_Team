# The Quant Noob — Idea Validation (SEAS Framework)
## Year 1: All 21 Recorded Videos

**Created:** 2026-05-11
**Framework:** SEAS (Spread of Users, Excitement, Audience Match, Simplicity)
**Replaces:** Idea-Validation-SEAS-10-May-2026.md

> **SEAS scoring:** Each dimension 1–10. Total /40.
> **S — Spread of Users:** How many *types* of people would click this — SWEs, ML engineers, students, finance people
> **E — Excitement:** Thumbnail potential, emotional pull, "I have to watch this" factor
> **A — Audience Match:** Directly relevant to The Quant Noob ICP (software engineer/ML person entering quant)
> **S2 — Simplicity:** How easy to package into a clear title without losing meaning
>
> **CCN Fit:** Core (series followers) | Casual (dips in sometimes) | New (first-time visitor)
> **Priority threshold:** ≥35 = priority launch. 30–34 = approved. <30 = needs reframe before use.
