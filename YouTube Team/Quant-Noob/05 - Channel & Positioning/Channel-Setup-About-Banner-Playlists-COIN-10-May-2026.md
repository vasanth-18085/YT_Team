### Tone Rules (COIN Output)

1. **Never use "guru" language.** No "I'll show you how to make money," no "the secret is," no "most people don't know."
2. **Always be specific about what you don't know.** Vagueness reads as fakery. Specific ignorance reads as honesty.
3. **Technical vocabulary is acceptable when defined on first use.** Define it once, then use it freely.
4. **Sentences should feel spoken, not written.** Read every line out loud. If you wouldn't say it to a friend, cut it.
5. **"We" only when genuinely collaborative.** Don't use "we" to fake community. Use "I" when it's just you.
6. **Enthusiasm is earned, not performed.** If something is genuinely interesting, explain why. Don't just say "this is amazing."
